About Eks Place

Eks Place is dedicated to providing the finest natural coconut oil products, crafted using traditional methods with great care. Our mission is to deliver purity, quality, and wellness to every home we serve.

Our Story

Serving Tradition Since 1983

We started as a small family-run mill, bringing the finest natural flours, spices, and oils to every kitchen. Our products are handcrafted with care, using traditional methods passed down through generations.

From pure coconut oil to millet mixes and homemade pickles, everything we make is fresh, natural, and packed with love for your family.
